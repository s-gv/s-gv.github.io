Sagar Gubbi


I'm a PhD student advised by Prof. Bharadwaj Amrutur at the Indian Institute Of Science. In a previous life, I worked on low-power circuits.

Academic Research

RNICU uses low-power sensors to remotely monitor physiological parameters of newborns in rural India.
All digital energy minimizer uses a fully synthesizable circuit that monitors energy consumption of sub-threshold digital circuits to dynamically track the Minimum Energy Point (MEP).
